Welcome to Unicon19 Dashboard

Brief Description:

Unicon 19 is a global event that brings together unicyclists from around the world to compete, share skills, and celebrate the unicycling community. The event features various competitions, workshops, and cultural exchanges, fostering a sense of unity and promoting the sport of unicycling.

Key Aspects:
• Global Participation: Attracts participants from numerous countries, highlighting the international appeal of unicycling.
• Diverse Competitions: Includes freestyle performances, races, and more, catering to different skill sets.
• Cultural Exchange: Hosted in South Korea, providing opportunities for cultural immersion and experience.
